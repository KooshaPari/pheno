## Design Context

### Users
Recruiters, collaborators, and future clients who need a fast read on Koosha Paridehpour's work.
The site also serves as a durable personal home for the author, with direct routes to projects,
contact, and legacy portfolio pages.

### Brand Personality
Quietly confident, technical, and precise. The tone should feel credible rather than promotional,
with a strong bias toward clarity, evidence, and good editorial rhythm.

### Aesthetic Direction
Warm editorial minimalism with systems-oriented structure. Use restrained color, sharp hierarchy,
and a real photographic anchor instead of abstract decoration. The site should feel like a crafted
studio portfolio, not a template.

### Design Principles
1. Lead with the person, then the work, then the links.
2. Keep the layout calm and legible on mobile and desktop.
3. Use one strong visual asset and let typography do the rest.
4. Make project pages and legacy forwards explicit and durable.
5. Favor evidence and specifics over marketing language.
